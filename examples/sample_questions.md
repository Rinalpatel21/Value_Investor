# Sample Questions for the BTC AI Trading Assistant

This document contains example questions you can ask the AI Trading Assistant.

---

# Portfolio

Ask about your current portfolio.

* Show my portfolio
* What is my portfolio value?
* How much cash do I have?
* How much Bitcoin do I own?
* What is my average buy price?
* Show my current holdings
* How much is my BTC worth?
* What percentage of my portfolio is invested in Bitcoin?

---

# Market Analysis

Ask about the current Bitcoin market.

* What is the current BTC price?
* Explain today's market conditions
* What market regime are we in?
* Is the market trending or ranging?
* What trading strategy is currently active?
* Explain the current strategy
* Is volatility high today?
* What does RSI indicate?
* What does ATR tell us?
* Is Bitcoin overbought or oversold?

---

# Trading Performance

Review how your trading strategy is performing.

* Show my trading performance
* Am I profitable?
* How much money have I made?
* What is my total return?
* Show my profit and loss
* What is my current PnL?
* How has my portfolio performed?
* Show my portfolio performance

---

# Trade History

View historical trading activity.

* Show recent trades
* Show paper orders
* What was my last trade?
* When did I last buy Bitcoin?
* When did I last sell Bitcoin?
* Show today's trading activity
* Display my latest transactions

---

# AI Decision Support

Ask the LLM for trading insights.

* Should I buy Bitcoin now?
* Should I sell Bitcoin now?
* Analyze my portfolio
* Explain today's recommendation
* Why did the AI recommend buying?
* Why did the AI recommend selling?
* Why is the AI holding?
* What would you do in the current market?
* Is now a good time to invest?
* Explain the current trading opportunity

---

# Risk Management

Understand portfolio risk.

* Is my portfolio at risk?
* How much risk am I taking?
* What is my exposure?
* Explain my position sizing
* Is my portfolio balanced?
* Why didn't the AI buy more Bitcoin?
* Why wasn't a trade executed?

---

# Strategy Questions

Learn about the hybrid trading system.

* Explain your trading strategy
* How does Dollar Cost Averaging work?
* How does swing trading work?
* What is ATR?
* What indicators are being used?
* How do you detect market regimes?
* What happens during a panic market?
* Explain the hybrid strategy

---

# Performance Metrics

Ask about key statistics.

* Show my return percentage
* What is my win rate?
* How many trades have I made?
* Show my recent performance
* What is my average buy price?
* How much cash is available?

# Supported Topics

The assistant can answer questions related to:

* Bitcoin (BTC)
* Cryptocurrency trading
* Portfolio management
* Trade history
* Market analysis
* Technical indicators (RSI, ATR, EMA, SMA)
* AI trading decisions
* Trading strategies
* Performance metrics
* Risk management
* Paper trading

---

# Unsupported Questions

The assistant is intentionally restricted and will politely decline unrelated requests, including:

* General knowledge
* Personal advice
* Politics
* Medical questions
* Programming unrelated to the trading project
* Homework
* Weather
* Sports
* Entertainment
* Personal opinions
